Cocktails

  • Betelgeuse

    Mezcal - Honey Simple Syrup - Beet Juice - Green Chartreuse - Lime Juice

    • 2oz mezcal (I prefer a light smokiness so it doesn't overpower the drink)
    • 0.5oz honey simple syrup (1:1)
    • 0.75oz beet juice (fresh beets juiced from a juicer)
    • 0.25oz green chartreuse
    • 0.5oz freshly squeezed lime juice
    • 0.5oz freshly squeezed lime juice

    Combine all ingredients into a shaker and shake with ice. Strain into a coupe glass.
